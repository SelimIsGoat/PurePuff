About Us

We all came together with this idea together in Las Vegas. We were all together in a casino in Vegas when all of a sudden a guy smoking fell to the ground. Later that day, we found out he had passed away due to a smoking related lung condition. After that, wasted no time in creating PurePuff. PurePuff is a diffuser that is used once someone is already addicted to smoking/vaping, and it stops the addiction by having the similar 100% natural scent/smell, such as fruit scenets. After all dedicating time to this project, we got sponsors and became a worldwide product.

Main Things/Importants

  • Helps Reduce Addiction from Smoking/Vaping
  • Help Build Stronger Lungs After They Were Damaged
  • Has different flavors/scents so addiction is reduced
